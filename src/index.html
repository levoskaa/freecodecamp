Rand al'Thor

The Dragon Reborn, Champion of the Light

Here's a list of Rand's achievements:

  • the most powerful channeler of any Age
  • rediscovered many long-forgotten weaves (Skimming, Travelling, Balefire, etc.)
  • cleansed Saidin, thus preventing any further male channelers from going mad
  • resealed the Dark One's prison
  • became a Blademaster, an acknowledged master of sword-based combat
  • being a Ta'veren he is a very important figure, around whom all of reality may be bent

"He is born again! I feel him! The Dragon takes his first breath on the slopes of Dragonmount! He is coming! He is coming! Light help us! Light help the world! He lies in the snow and cries like the thunder! He burns like the sun!"

Gitara Moroso, Keeper of the Chronicles
